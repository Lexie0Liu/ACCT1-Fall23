Text as Material and Artifact Project Brief

Reference & Inspiration

This project draws inspiration from OpenAI's DALL·E and the high-quality photography available on Unsplash. DALL·E's ability to transform textual prompts into creative visual outputs showcases the potential of AI in generative art, while Unsplash serves as a benchmark for authentic and realistic imagery. By comparing these two sources, the project aims to explore the intersection of technology and reality, highlighting differences in style, focus, and creative interpretation.

Game Production

Final Result

I used a light color as the background. On the left is the Unsplash API. After clicking the search button, real-world photos will appear. On the right, AI-generated images will be generated based on the words you provided, and prompts will be given during the search process. Finally, a comparative analysis result of the two pictures will be generated.

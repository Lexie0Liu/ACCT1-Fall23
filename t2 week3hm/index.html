Week3 Assignment--Archeology of the Futures

Reference & Inspiration

In my vision for the future, the world I contemplate leans towards extinctionism. In a society where resources are increasingly scarce and the gap between rich and poor widens, the wealthy control the resources. Therefore, I want to take 'The Matrix' as my inspiration, where the protagonists, among others, live their lives in 'pods,' always believing that to be the real world.

Initial thoughts on future scenarios

In this era of increasingly scarce energy resources and rapid technological advancement, "Dream Pod" represents an unprecedented breakthrough. It is more than just a product; it is a gateway to the future, an exploration of an ideal lifestyle. This sleep pod employs the most advanced, cutting-edge technology, allowing users to experience a flawless virtual utopian world while they sleep.

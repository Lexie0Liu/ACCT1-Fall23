Digital Materiality

Project Brief

For this project, I will use TouchDesigner to create a total of 6 different types of visual effects, and include 20 unique variations.

01. Particle Motion

For the first variation, I changed the "fractal" and "noise" settings, and added the "bloom" image filter. I created a total of 5 different versions.

02. Water Ripple

For the second iteration, I experimented with "edge" and "blur", making a total of 5 different edges and blurs. I also adjusted the "seed" and "period" within the "noise" function to create 3 variations.

03. Butterfly

For the third image, I adjusted the gradient color using the "ramp" tool, and experimented with "feedback", "transform", and "level". Within the "level" option, I manipulated "Gamma" and "Invert" to achieve a floating effect. I created 3 variations.

04. Sphere Dynamics

For my fourth design, I created a sphere and used noise and fractal functions. I also added lines and experimented with the transform function to create 5 variations. I found it interesting to change the dots to lines.

05. Acid Liquid

For my fifth design, I created an effect of acid liquid. Also played with "feedback", "noise", "level", and using "null". Total 3 variations.

06. Donut

For my final design, I created a donut and box particles and they will interact with the music I inserted. The music I played is from my favorite DJ illenium.
